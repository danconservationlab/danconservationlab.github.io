# Research

## Migratory Birds & Direct Mortality
Understanding when, where, and why migratory birds experience direct mortality in coastal systems.

## Human–Wildlife Coexistence
Studying interactions between wildlife and aquaculture, especially in the context of razor clam farming and mudflat ecosystems.

## Coastal Social–Ecological Systems
Analyzing how human activities reshape intertidal and coastal communities, and how conservation solutions can be co-designed.

## Remote Sensing & Ecological Monitoring
Using drones, satellite data, and modeling to understand habitat change and ecological processes.
